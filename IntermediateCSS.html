Google Fonts

  1. Use the search bar to find the font of your choosing (or simply browse the directory for a font)
  2. Click the red circle with the plus sign to the right of the font name
  3. Now click the grey tab that appears in the lower right corner of the screen
  4. Click the "customize" tab and select any of the options you need
  5. Click the "embed" tab and copy the link beneath the "standard" option
  6. Paste this link tag into the < head > of your project, before the link to your custom CSS

CSS Box Model

Explanation of the different parts:

  • Content - The content of the box, where text and images appear
  • Padding - Clears an area around the content. The padding is transparent
  • Border - A border that goes around the padding and content
  • Margin - Clears an area outside the border. The margin is transparent

The box model allows us to add a border around elements, and to define space between elements.
